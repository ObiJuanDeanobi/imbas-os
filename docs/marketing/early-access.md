# Early access and Patreon copy

This is draft copy for early-access/supporter messaging. Do not publish until approved.

## Core framing

Imbas OS is free and local-first. Patreon supports development and gives early access to experimental builds and behind-the-scenes progress. Team/business features will be a separate cloud/workspace product later.

## Short announcement draft

I’m building Imbas OS: a local-first home base for AI agents.

The idea is simple: AI work should not disappear when the chat ends. Imbas OS captures artifacts, wiki updates, run history, context packs, snapshots, and human approvals in one private workspace.

The local core is intended to be free/open source. Patreon is for people who want to support development, test early builds, and help shape the roadmap before public 1.0.

## Patreon intro draft

### Support Imbas OS

Imbas OS is a local-first workspace for serious AI agent work: artifacts, memory, living wiki pages, Runledger history, context packs, snapshots, and human review.

Your support helps fund development while keeping the core local-first and open.

Patreon supporters get early updates, preview builds, and a closer feedback loop as the project moves toward public 1.0.

## Boundary copy

Patreon is not a business support plan and does not include custom work, SLAs, team support, or managed hosting. Those belong to the future paid cloud/workspace product.

## Plain-language promise

The local-first core should be genuinely useful without paying. Paid plans should add convenience, hosted reliability, collaboration, compliance, and business controls — not take away ownership.
